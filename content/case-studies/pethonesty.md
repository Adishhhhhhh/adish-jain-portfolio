## The problem mechanism

- Most dog supplements are built on a base of corn, wheat, soy, cellulose, and palm oil.
- These ingredients are cheap, standard, and technically pass "natural" labeling requirements.
- They also irritate the gut lining daily, compete directly with nutrient absorption pathways, and disrupt fat-soluble vitamin uptake (A, D, E).
- Since approximately 70% of a dog's immune response originates in the digestive system, filler-induced gut irritation doesn't just block individual nutrients.
- It degrades the central engine of immunity, digestion, energy, and skin health simultaneously. The dog is swallowing an expensive placebo.

## The solution mechanism

- PetHonesty strips every blocking ingredient out first, then builds on the Pure Vitality Blend, a proprietary base of pumpkin fiber, prebiotics, and antioxidants.
- Pumpkin fiber supports gut motility and feeds beneficial bacteria without irritating the gut wall.
- The same gut wall that processes the fiber is the one absorbing glucosamine into the joints, shuttling omega-3s to the skin, and delivering B vitamins to the nervous system.
- The gut stops fighting the supplement. It starts processing it.
- The analogy that makes it stick: filling a bathtub with someone partially blocking the drain. You keep pouring water in. The tub never fills. The problem was never the water.

## Creative Assets

### Direct Response And Creative Principles Applied Across All Assets

- Every script opens on the dog parent's emotional reality first, the product enters only after the pain or tension is already established.
- The filler absorption mechanism is the core belief shift this stack is designed to create. Every script either leads with it, builds to it, or assumes it as the foundation.
- Social proof is deployed as specific numbers, not vague claims: 250,000+ subscribers, 300,000+ reviews, AKC recognition, PhD formulation. No generic "customers love it" language.
- "Natural" is never used as a positive signal. Every script where it appears treats it as a label loophole, not a safety guarantee.
- Format follows emotional register. Heavier emotional scripts use UGC confessional. Mechanism-heavy scripts use educational DR. Humor scripts use skit or character format. No format is applied by default.
- The CTA always closes on the dog parent's desire, not the product's features. The last line earns the click emotionally, not logically.
- Every script is written with attention span as a hard constraint. No VSL exceeds 120 seconds. Most run 30 to 60 seconds. No filler, no preamble, no recap. Every line either earns attention or loses it.
