### The Problem MitoQ Solves

**Why Standard Supplements Fail:**

When you take standard antioxidants (CoQ10, NAD+ precursors, vitamins C/E), they face a critical barrier:

**The Mitochondrial Membrane:**

- Mitochondria are protected by a highly selective double phospholipid bilayer
- This membrane has an electrochemical gradient (-150 to -180mV negative charge)
- It actively repels most molecules, including standard antioxidants
- Only allows specific molecules with the right structure and charge to enter

**The Result:**
Standard antioxidants → can't penetrate membrane → float in bloodstream → eliminated via urine → **no cellular protection**

**This explains customer experience:**
"I tried CoQ10 for years and felt nothing" (61% of reviews)

The supplement wasn't "bad" - it just never reached the destination.

### Unique Mechanism (How MitoQ's TPP+ Delivery System Works)

**The Breakthrough: Triphenylphosphonium (TPP+) Cation**

**Step 1: Positive Charge Attraction**

- MitoQ molecule is conjugated with a TPP+ cation (positive charge)
- Mitochondrial matrix has negative charge (-150 to -180mV)
- Opposite charges attract → mitochondria actively PULL MitoQ inside

**Step 2: Membrane Penetration**

- The positive charge acts as an "electrochemical magnet"
- TPP+ carrier crosses BOTH mitochondrial membranes
- Exploits the mitochondria's own electrical gradient

**Step 3: Concentration Inside Mitochondria**

- MitoQ accumulates at **100-1000x higher levels** inside mitochondria vs standard antioxidants
- Concentrates precisely where 90% of free radicals are generated
- Stays localized at the source of damage

**Step 4: Continuous Protection**

- Once inside, MitoQ integrates into the inner mitochondrial membrane
- Cycles between active (reduced) and inactive (oxidized) forms
- Provides **continuous free radical neutralization** 24/7
- Doesn't "use up" and disappear - keeps working
